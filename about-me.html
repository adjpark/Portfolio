ABOUT ME

Hi, My Name is Andrew Park

I am a web developer / graphic designer and a recent graduate from Digital Design & Development Program at BCIT. Digital Design & Development consists of web development, graphic design, and marketing. With this program, I have achieved great exposure on a variety of process of creating and promoting web applications. However, my interest and passion were more attracted to the development side of creating an app. Thus, I decided to become a web developer with supporting skills in graphic design.

Background

I was born and raised in Korea. I was fortunate enough to come to Vancouver to improve my education. Furthermore, I had a chance to live on the east side of Canada due to my family moving to Ontario.

My passion for the web development was ignited when I first took an introductory HTML course that was provided at my high school. The course was simple; we would create a simple website with HTML and customize it however we liked using CSS. This was one of my favorite class during my high school period. If I have not taken this course, I would not have found my passion and interest that kept me going till now.

What I love about climbing are the moments when you reach the top. My highest achievement in my climbing career would be finishing a 5.11 route. Additionally, I love bouldering as well. If I have to pick between climbing and bouldering, I would prefer bouldering. The reason why I would pick bouldering is that I like the challenge of performing difficult moves.

On top of rock climbing, I also love to snowboard during the winter season. Although I am not proficient at it compared to rock climbing, I enjoy snowboarding given that there are amazing mountains in Vancouver.
