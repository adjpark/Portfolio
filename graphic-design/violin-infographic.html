VIOLIN INFOGRAPHIC

Adobe Illustrator

Description

The project was done to learn more about creating logos and icons with illustrator. Additionally, learning about PPI and a proper way of importing real pictures to an illustrator file. For my topic, I choose violin because I used to play violin when I was a kid, and i want to show the readers how intriguing the design of the violin is.

Process

I started the project with gathering all the asset such as violin facts, a picture of different types of violins, 3d exploded view of violin and other information. After gathering all the assets, I started with simple placeholders to organize the content and decide on the location of where the assets will be. Moving on, I created the illustrated violin and some key icons such as the different sizes of a violin. Lastly, I compiled all of the content accordingly to the placeholders.

Summary

From this project I was able to practice most of my illustrator skills such as creating shapes, drawing paths, pathfinder, paragraph and character styles.

Project Details

Category: Graphic Design

Client: BCIT School Project

Date: 2016 April

Project Type: Individual Project

Purpose:

  • Use illustrator to create an infographic on a topic of your choice.
  • Have a proper font and color choices for your infographic.
  • Create different icons with illustrator.

Outcome:

  • Create icons that match the theme of your project.
  • Import images appropriately to the illustrator files.
  • Arranging content appropriately to enhance the readability of the infographic.
